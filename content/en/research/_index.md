## AI for Catalyst
We integrate AI-driven inverse materials design with real experimental validation to accelerate the discovery and optimization of high-performance catalysts.
## Autonomous Experimentation
We build autonomous experimental platforms to enable the automation and high-throughput optimization of catalytic materials research, improving both experimental efficiency and result reliability.
## Catalysis
Focus on heterogeneous catalysis and single-atom catalysis, exploring the construction of active sites and reaction mechanisms in key catalytic processes.
## Electrochemistry
We investigate electrochemical interfacial processes and energy conversion mechanisms to develop efficient electrocatalytic materials and reaction systems.
